13 / Risk & Mitigation
Seed Deck · Confidential
Named, Not Hidden

The four risks we lose sleep over, and what we do about them.

Honesty builds credibility. We would rather name the risks our investors will ask about than have them surface during diligence.

Risk 01 · Technical

BRC-2.0 immaturity

Programmable Bitcoin via BRC-2.0 is new. Indexers, tooling, and smart contract behavior are unproven at scale. A standard regression could delay launch or constrain features.

Mitigation

Multi-indexer strategy (UniSat + Best in Slot + custom) removes single point of failure. Conservative TVL caps at launch. Multi-orbit architecture allows pivot to Stacks/Rootstock/Arch if BRC-2.0 timelines slip.

Risk 02 · Market

Crypto drawdown

A broad crypto market crash could devastate TVL and user engagement overnight. Memecoin categories are first to bleed in a downturn.

Mitigation

No-loss game modes retain users through downturns. Principal is preserved, only yield is at stake. Treasury yield diversifies revenue beyond fee capture. Conservative burn rate extends runway.

Risk 03 · Audience

Chinese retail concentration

Phase 1 leans heavily on a single audience geography. Regulatory or platform-level disruption to that audience could stall growth.

Mitigation

Phase 2 global expansion begins within 6 months of launch. Anon-friendly architecture and jurisdiction-neutral design. No KYC gate at the protocol layer.

Risk 04 · Regulatory

Gaming & DeFi regulation

DeFi gaming sits in an ambiguous space across North American and EU frameworks. Misclassification as gambling or securities could restrict markets.

Mitigation

Skill-based game design minimizes gambling classification risk. BlockSkunk advisory relationship (in discussions) provides crypto-native compliance strategy from day one. Multi-jurisdiction structure.

Risk · Transparent about what's hard
