The Process

How ACI works in 5 steps.

1

Discovery & Positioning Audit

We start with a 60-minute discovery call and a full audit of your existing content, ICP, and competitive positioning. We identify exactly where you're invisible in AI search — and what it will take to change that.

2

Content Strategy Build

We build a 90-day content strategy mapped to your ICP's actual buying questions. Every topic is chosen because it answers what your buyers are already asking ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google — not because it sounds good in a content meeting.

3

Production & Publishing

We write, edit, format, and publish — across LinkedIn, X, your blog, and TikTok. You review (optional) and approve. We handle every platform's native format so content doesn't feel like it was copied and pasted from somewhere else.

4

GEO Optimization Loop

Every piece of content is structured to be cited by AI search engines. We use direct question-answer formatting, authoritative definitions, structured data, and consistent brand entity signals across platforms — the four signals that determine whether ChatGPT mentions you or your competitor.

5

Feedback & Compounding

Monthly: we review what's performing, what's getting saved (not just liked), and what the engagement data says about what your market actually wants to buy. Every month the content gets sharper. That's the compound effect — and it's why clients who start at Signal often move to Command within six months.
