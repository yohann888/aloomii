How it works

AI scans. Humans judge. You act.

01

Right place, right time, right relationship

Your CRM, your socials (LinkedIn/X), your future clients & partners. We connect to the signals that matter, not just what's in your database, but what's happening around your relationships in real time.

AI detects what humans miss

Our agents monitor hundreds of touchpoints simultaneously: client activity, competitor moves, life events, job changes, market shifts, engagement signals.
