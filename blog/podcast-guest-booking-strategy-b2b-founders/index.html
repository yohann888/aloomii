Podcast Guest Booking for B2B Founders: How to Land the Right Guests (Who Become Clients)

TL;DR

3 guest buckets: ideal clients, referral sources, ecosystem partners. Podcast outreach converts 35-40% cold. Pre-interview brief turns good conversations into great ones. 30/60/90-day cadence turns relationships into pipeline.

Most podcast hosts chase fame. The founders building real businesses with their podcasts chase alignment. There's a fundamental difference between booking a guest who gets you downloads and booking a guest who gets you pipeline. This distinction is the foundation of the complete system this post is part of.

Why Most Hosts Book the Wrong Guests

The instinct to book impressive, famous guests is wrong for B2B founders. A celebrity guest gets you downloads. An ICP-aligned guest. a potential client, referral source, or connector to your buyers. gets you a warm relationship and a tracked contact in your CRM.

The Guest Selection Framework

Bucket 1: Ideal Clients. People who match your ICP and could become customers. Book them, interview them, build the relationship before you ever pitch.

Bucket 2: Referral Sources. People whose clients are your ICP. One referral from a guest with a strong network in your vertical is worth 6 months of cold outreach.

Bucket 3: Ecosystem Partners. People whose audiences are your ICP. When the episode publishes, you get distributed to exactly the buyers you're trying to reach.

Outreach That Converts

Podcast invitations convert at 35-40% cold. far higher than cold email. because you're offering value, not asking for it. Template: 'I host [Podcast Name] focused on [topic]. I've been following [specific thing they've done] and think your perspective on [specific angle] would be valuable for our audience. Would you be open to a 45-minute conversation?' No pitch. No deck. The platform is the value.

The Pre-Interview Intelligence Brief

Before every guest: their current business and recent news, their public positions, mutual connections, and three questions nobody else has asked them. Guests who feel well-researched become advocates. they share the episode and recommend you.

The Post-Interview Pipeline

Day 0-7: personal thank-you + episode link. Day 30: performance metric + check-in. Day 60: value-add with no ask. Day 90: natural pipeline conversation. The conversion happens naturally when the relationship is real.
