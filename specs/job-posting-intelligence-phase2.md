# Phase 2 — Job Posting Intelligence Inside Signal Scout

## Goal
Add job-posting intelligence as a new Signal Scout input, not a separate product.

## Scope
- weekly manual-first scan
- prospect hiring signals
- narrow competitor hiring signals
- routes into Signal Scout, CRM tags, and outreach queue

## Prospect roles to watch
- Head of AI / Director of AI
- Director of Digital Transformation
- CTO (new hire)
- VP Operations
- Marketing Technology Manager
- Data Engineer / Analyst (first hire)
- Innovation Lead
- roles mentioning AI, automation, digital transformation

## Competitor roles to watch
- engineering roles that reveal roadmap
- sales roles that reveal GTM expansion
- leadership roles that reveal strategic changes

## Outputs
- prospect signal rows tagged `hiring-signal`
- competitor notes added to Signal Scout report
- top prospects routed to outreach queue

## Rule
This stays tightly ICP-scoped. No broad job scraping.
