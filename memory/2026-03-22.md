## Lessons Learned
- Hero text with specificity converts better than a claim. "12 conversations: partnership intros, podcast bookings, warm meetings" > "12 qualified conversations." The breakdown is the credibility signal.
- Anti-positioning sections ("what we don't do") remove the most common objections before the reader raises them. Include in all future landing pages.
- The cat origin story is more valuable than any positioning statement for first impressions. Use it every time without over-engineering it.
- Brand soul documents should be updated immediately when brand positioning locks — not retroactively. SOUL.md was missing the Sprint positioning, ICPs, and proof points until this session. Fix: update SOUL.md same session as any positioning lock.
