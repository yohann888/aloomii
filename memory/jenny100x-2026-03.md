## Fatal Flaws (all 3 models agreed — address these first)

1. **Zero revenue with zero proof.** The ICP raise to $50K+ MRR makes the trust gap worse, not better. These founders have options and won't pay $3K/mo to an unproven operator with no case studies.
2. **9 "founding partner pilots" with none confirmed paying is a wishlist, not a pipeline.** Until money moves, these are warm contacts at best.
3. **Infrastructure is outpacing selling.** 15+ AI agents, 66 blog articles, deck factories, signal scouts, Village.do. Worth exactly $0 until someone pays. The ratio of tooling to revenue is dangerously inverted.
4. **Signal Scout finds leads but Yohann is DMing manually on Reddit.** The entire Aloomii value proposition is "we run GTM so you don't have to." The founder doing outreach by hand defeats the product thesis.

## Consensus Moves (all 3 models independently recommended)

### #1 — The Proof Machine
All three models opened with this. The specific framing differed; the conclusion was identical.

Aloomii has never run The Sprint for anyone but itself. No case study. No testimonial. No before/after. Every day spent building more tooling without a paying client is a day the core thesis remains untested.

**The fix:**
- Pick 1-3 paying design partners from the founding roster (discounted but paid, explicit case study rights)
- Document the Sprint log publicly: week 0 state, weekly inputs/outputs, end-of-90-day results
- Publish the Jim Rogers story as a documented relationship sequence ("here are the 14 touches over 17 years, here is how the system would have handled 12 of them") — not just a name drop

One real case study is worth more than 66 blog posts.

### #2 — The Signal-to-Close Loop
All three models flagged the disconnect between Signal Scout output and actual conversion. 150-300 qualified signals/month with manual Reddit DMs is a broken funnel.

**The fix:**
- Wire Signal Scout → Village.do path detection → Gamma deck auto-draft → human review → send
- Track the funnel in PostgreSQL: signal → first touch → reply → conversation → proposal → close (this table doesn't exist yet)
- Test manually on 10 leads first. Time each step. Measure conversion at each stage before automating.
- At 10% signal-to-conversation, 150 signals/month = 15 conversations = 3 new clients/month at 20% close rate
