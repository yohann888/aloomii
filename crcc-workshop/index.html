03 / Let's clear the air

You've probably heard a lot of things about AI.

Myth

"It's going to replace your job."

Myth

"It's going to write all your content."

Myth

"If you're not using it, you're falling behind."

What's True

AI is a tool. Like email was a tool. Like spreadsheets were a tool.

It doesn't replace you, or your employees. It handles the repetitive work so you can focus on the work that actually makes you money: the relationships, the conversations, the trust.

Today I'll show you exactly where AI helps, and exactly where it will hurt you if you let it.

04 / Thing 1
01
What AI Does Well

AI can draft your communications.

Every week you write:

  • Follow-up emails to clients
  • Social media posts
  • Responses to inquiries
  • Thank-you notes after meetings
  • Quotes and proposals
  • Newsletter updates
⏱ What used to take 45 minutes staring at a blank screen now takes 5 minutes of editing.
Live Demo

Drafting an email

Open ChatGPT or Claude. Paste this:

"Write a follow-up email to a client I met at the chamber networking event last week. We talked about their business looking to update their website. Keep it warm and personal, not salesy. Two paragraphs max."

Then read it. Edit it. Make it sound like you. Then send it yourself.

THE KEY: AI gives you the starting point. You add the personality.
That's 80% of the work done in 10 seconds.
05 / Thing 2
02
What AI Does Well

AI can monitor your market for you.

Right now, do you know:

  • When a competitor gets a bad Google review?
  • When a local business posts a job listing?
  • When a client's company appears in the news?
  • When a new business opens in your area?

Most of you find out by accident. Someone mentions it at a chamber event. You stumble on it weeks later. AI can watch for these signals automatically and tell you every morning.

Live Demo

Free monitoring (3 minutes)

Step 1. Go to google.com/alerts

Step 2. Enter your competitor's business name. Hit create.

Step 3. Enter your top 5 client company names. Hit create.

Step 4. Enter "Caledonia business" or "Haldimand County business."

Now you get an email every time Google finds new information about these topics.

FREE · 3 MINUTES · KNOW BEFORE COMPETITORS DO

06 / Thing 3
03
What AI Does Well

AI can organize your client knowledge.

Where your client notes live now:

  • Sticky notes on your monitor
  • Email threads from 6 months ago
  • Your memory (good luck)
  • A spreadsheet you started but stopped updating
  • A notebook you can't find

AI can turn scattered notes into a system. You talk to it the way you'd talk to an assistant.

Live Demo

Organizing client notes

Paste your messy notes. Then say:

"Here are my rough notes from 5 client meetings this week. Organize them into a clean summary for each client with: key topics discussed, action items, and when I should follow up."

You get back a clean, structured, searchable record. 2 minutes of work.

✓ Doing this manually takes 30 minutes — and most people skip it entirely.
07 / Now the important part

Those 3 things will save you time every single week.

They're free. They're easy. You can start today.

A Word of Warning

Some people will try to sell you a $15,000 AI agent that promises to replace your receptionist or answer all your calls. That's not what we're talking about today. Today is about three things you can do yourself, this week, for free.

But here's where most AI advice gets dangerous.

There are 2 things AI should never do for your business. Get them wrong, and it won't just waste your time —
it will cost you clients.

08 / Don't do this #1

Never send a message without a human reading it.

A company set up an AI system to send follow-up emails automatically. The AI drafted a cheerful outreach to a prospect. Great personalization. Perfect timing.

One problem: that prospect's company had just gone through layoffs. They were dealing with letting people go. And the AI sent a perky "excited to connect!" email right into the middle of their worst week.

The AI didn't know. It couldn't know. A human would have read the room. The AI read the spreadsheet.

The Rule
AI drafts. Humans decide.
09 / Don't do this #2 + The framework

Never let AI replace the relationship.

Your business runs on relationships. AI can prepare you for the conversation. AI cannot have the conversation.

80%
AI Handles

Repetitive work.

Drafting messages. Monitoring signals. Organizing notes. Scheduling follow-ups. Data entry. Research. Formatting. Distribution.

20%
You Handle

What closes deals.

Conversations. Judgment calls. Relationship moments. The intuition to sense when something is off. The reason a client trusts you and stays with you.

That 20% is where your revenue actually comes from.
AI just makes sure you spend more time there instead of drowning in admin work.

Your week, starting Monday.

No software to buy. No subscription needed. No consultant required.
