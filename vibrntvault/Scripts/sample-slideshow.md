# Slideshow Script: Minimalist Aesthetic Collection

## Framework: Hook → Show → Close

### HOOK (0-2 sec)
**Visual:** Slow pan across a clean white desk — MacBook, coffee, a single plant. Soft blur.
**Text overlay:** "things that make my space feel like me"
**Sound:** "Snowfall" instrumental fades in softly

### SHOW (3-8 sec)
**Slide 1 (1.5 sec):** Close-up of sticker pack laid out on a marble surface. Gentle zoom in.
**Text overlay:** "the stickers → Plant Mom Pack"

**Slide 2 (1.5 sec):** Stickers applied to a laptop and water bottle. Real desk setting, not flat lay.
**Text overlay:** "on everything I own"

**Slide 3 (2 sec):** Affirmation hoodie draped over a chair next to a journal and candle. Warm tones.
**Text overlay:** "the hoodie → You Are Enough"

**Slide 4 (1 sec):** Someone pulling the hoodie on (no face — just hands and fabric). Cozy.
**Text overlay:** "for the days you need a reminder"

### CLOSE (last 2 sec)
**Visual:** Final wide shot of the full styled desk with products visible.
**Text overlay:** "VIBRNT ✨ link in bio"
**Sound:** Music fades out.

---

## Production Notes
- All footage is **b-roll only** — no face, no talking
- Source from: your own phone footage OR licensed clips (Pexels, Canva Pro)
- Color grade: warm, slightly desaturated — think Kinfolk magazine
- Transitions: slow dissolve or Ken Burns (zoom) only. No swipes or flashes.
- Edit in CapCut — total runtime 10-12 seconds
- Export 9:16 at 1080x1920
- Font for text overlays: clean serif (Playfair Display) or minimal sans (Futura)
