# Self-Film Script: Cottagecore Reading Nook

## Framework: Hook → Show → Close

### HOOK (0-2 sec)
**Visual:** Camera starts tight on an old book spine. Pull back slowly.
**Audio:** Trending cottagecore sound fades in.
**Text overlay:** "POV: your reading nook just got an upgrade"

### SHOW (3-8 sec)
**Visual:** Jenny/Yohann wearing "One More Chapter" tee, sitting in a window seat with natural light. Stack of books beside them. Tote bag hanging on a hook nearby with a book peeking out.
**Audio:** Sound continues. No talking needed.
**Text overlay (staggered):**
- "the tee: One More Chapter" (3 sec)
- "the tote: for your farmer's market book haul" (5 sec)
**Action:** Casually pick up coffee mug, sip, go back to reading. Relaxed. Not performed.

### CLOSE (last 2 sec)
**Visual:** Close-up of the tee text with soft focus background.
**Text overlay:** "link in bio 📖"
**Audio:** Sound fades.

## Production Notes
- Film in **natural light only** — golden hour or overcast window
- Shoot **vertical 9:16**
- No tripod needed — handheld adds warmth
- Outfit: the featured tee + relaxed bottoms (jeans or linen pants)
- Props: real books (visible spines add texture), coffee/tea mug, the tote
- Edit in CapCut — 2 cuts max, keep it feeling like one continuous moment
